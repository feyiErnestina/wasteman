What We Do

Waste Segregation

Recycling Of Waste

About Us

Our vision is to have a Green-Earth.

Our core motive is to reduce environmental pollution caused by waste. With enviromental pollution being a major factor and contributor of global warming, We are poised to reducing its effects and greening the earth. We affirm our stance on meeting the following Sustainable Development Goals(SDG's):

  • SDG's 6 : Clean Water and Good Sanitation.
  • SDG's 14: Life Below Water.
  • SDG's 15: Life On Land.

Services

We are providing the following services

Waste Segregation

Separation of waste into the various types eg. Plastics, metalic, Electronic, Paper and Organic Wastes.

Residential Waste Pick-up

Timely, Convenience and Routine pick-ups of waste from Households

Business Waste Pick-up

Timely, Convenience and Routine pick-ups of waste from institutions

Event Clean-Ups

Offer Sanitation services for Events like Seminars, Forums, Parties, Weddings, Funerals etc.

Plastic Waste Pick-Ups and Collection

Plastic waste pick-ups as well as community based plastic buy-back services.

Waste Education

Educating the Society on segregation, recycling and the impact waste pollution has incurred on the earth.
